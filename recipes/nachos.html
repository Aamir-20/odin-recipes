Cheesy Nachos

Description

Enjoy this quick and delicious cheesy nacho snack, perfect for any gathering or solo treat! Made with crunchy tortilla chips, spicy salsa, melted cheese, and a kick of jalapeños or chilies, this recipe is both simple and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 200g Nachos/Tortilla Chips
  • 300g Hot Salsa Dip
  • Mild Cheese (as much as you'd like!)
  • Handful of Jalapeños or 1 Chili (sliced, seeds preserved)

Steps

  1. Spread nachos on a baking tray.
  2. Pour salsa evenly over the chips.
  3. Slice chilies or jalapeños, keeping the seeds for extra heat.
  4. Add some chili slices to the tray, then sprinkle cheese over everything.
  5. Top with remaining chilies and seeds.
  6. Bake at 160-180°C fir 20 minutes, or until the cheese melts to a golden hue.

Serve hot and enjoy your cheesy nachos!
