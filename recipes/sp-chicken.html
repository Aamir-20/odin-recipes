Salt and Pepper Chicken

Description

This recipe creates a flavorful, crispy salt and pepper chicken perfect as a snack or main dish. Follow the steps to achieve a golden-brown coating with a deliciously spiced kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 large chicken breast, cut into small cubes
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp soy sauceco
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp crushed ginger
  • 1 red chili, chopped
  • ¼ tsp five-spice powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into small cubes.
  2. In a bowl, mix chicken cubes with ½ tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper, and 2 tbsp soy sauce.
  3. Add 1 egg to the chicken mixture and stir until well incorporated.
  4. Add 3 tbsp cornstarch to the chicken and mix thoroughly to coat.
  5. Heat oil in a pan for shallow frying.
  6. Place chicken pieces individually into the pan and cook on medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  7. In the same pan, add 1 tbsp of oil.
  8. Add the crushed garlic, crushed ginger, and chopped red chili to the pan. Sauté for about a minute until aromatic.
  9. Return the chicken pieces to the pan.
  10. Add ¼ tsp five-spice powder, a pinch of salt, and a dash of black pepper. Stir-fry well until everything is evenly coated.
  11. Once the chicken is golden brown and well mixed with the seasoning, remove it from the pan.

Serve your salt and pepper chicken hot, with a side of dipping sauce if desired!
