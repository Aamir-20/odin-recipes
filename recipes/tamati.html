Tamati

Description

This recipe yield enough for 7 people, with about 14-16 servings. It's a spicy, savory tomato-based dish perfect as a side or main.

Ingredients

  • 5 cans of plum tomatoes
  • 2 tsp green chili paste (or 1 frozen green chilli block)
  • Half cube garlic paste
  • 1.2 tsp salt (adjust if needed)
  • Half tsp addar powder
  • 3 tsp red chili powder
  • 4 tsp coriander powder
  • Pinch of red crushed chili
  • Pinch of cumin seeds (jiru)
  • 4 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Blend the 5 cans of tomatoes until smooth.
  2. In a deep pan (curry chumpcho), heat 4 tablespoons of oil. Add the blended tomatoes, cover with a lid, and set the heat to low.
  3. After 10 minutes, add 2 teaspoons of green chili paste and Â½ cube of garlic paste.
  4. Season with 1.2 teaspoons of salt, 0.5 teaspoon of ginger powder, 3 teaspoons of red chili powder, and 4 teaspoons of coriander powder.
  5. Add a pinch of red crushed chili and cumin seeds.
  6. Let the mixture simmer, stirring regularly to avoid sticking.
  7. Taste and add more salt if necessary.

Serve your tamati warm for a flavorful, spiced delight!
